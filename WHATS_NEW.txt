┌──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ 🚀 HOW TO USE                                                            │
└──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

1. SERVERS CURRENTLY RUNNING
   ✓ Backend: http://127.0.0.1:5000
   ✓ Frontend: http://localhost:5173

2. OPEN THE APP
   → Visit: http://localhost:5173
   → Click "Get Started" on homepage

3. COMPLETE 5-STEP FORM
   
   STEP 1 - Basic Info
   • Enter your name, age, education level
   • Add your current role/status
   
   STEP 2 - Interests
   • Check all areas that interest you
   • Options: Tech, Design, Business, Science, Arts
   
   STEP 3 - Core Skills
   • Rate 6 skills from 1-5
   • Skills: Communication, Creativity, Logic, Problem Solving,
            Teamwork, Leadership
   
   STEP 4 - Technical Skills
   • Rate 4 technical skills from 0-5 (0 = no experience)
   • Skills: Programming, Data Analysis, Writing, Public Speaking
   
   STEP 5 - Goals & Preferences
   • Enter academic performance (0-100)
   • Select personality type (Analytical/Creative/Social)
   • Choose work preference (Team/Independent/Mixed)
   • Pick career goal (Growth/Stability/Creativity/Impact/etc.)

4. VIEW RESULTS
   • See top 3 career matches
   • View confidence scores
   • Click "Details →" for full career info
   • Explore salary, growth, skills needed
   • Click "Learn More Online" for research
